The Research Single

Group-level Analysis of Relations Between Resting-State Functional Connectivity and Arithmetic Ability using CONN

Selected for a merit-based work-study at MTSU, I co-authored a neuroimaging study with Professor Xin Yang examining how resting-state brain connectivity relates to arithmetic ability — analyzing group-level fMRI data with the CONN toolbox to find connectivity patterns tied to mathematical performance.

The paper was published at the 2022 International Conference on Computational Science and Computational Intelligence (CSCI), and I presented the findings to the American Council on Science & Education.
